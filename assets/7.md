February 2021 — GameStop and AMC are blowing up, and I got curious: can internet hype predict prices? The tricky part was measuring hype. So I built an app that scans all activity on Reddit's [r/cryptocurrency](https://www.reddit.com/r/CryptoCurrency/) and tracks how often each coin gets mentioned. Exposed it through an API, built a simple frontend, and made a Reddit bot to keep the community updated.

The fun challenge: during peak hours, the subreddit fires up to 15 comments per second. Every comment needed to be ingested, scanned for coin mentions, and pushed to the database — all on a small machine. The database is now over 15 million comments. Keeping them around for someday 😁.

The app got some traction — over 100,000 unique visitors total, roughly 100–250 a day.
